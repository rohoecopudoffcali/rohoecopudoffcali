About the Government

Government Type: Kakistocracy

The Kakistocracy of Rohoecopudoffcali is administered under the principles established by the Constitution of the Republic and the Pebble Clause. Executive authority is exercised through the Office of the President and the Council of Ministries.

The Government remains committed to responsible administration, institutional stability, and the continued stewardship of national interests.
